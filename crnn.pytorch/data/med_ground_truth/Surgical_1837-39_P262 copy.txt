		Opthalmia .         164								P263 
Jan Morrison aged 21. Born in Ireland
seaman, admitted May 17th 1838
with slight conjunctivitis- there 
is redness + he complains of pain
in the ball of the eye. He
thinks it came from scraping
of the mast of a vessel upon
which he was at work the
applied eye was which relieved
it
Treatment:
	Aqua fontana applied to the eye;
bowels opened by neutral
salt + put upon a vegetable 
diet.
May 19th  Discharged Cured.
	Jas. B. Gould M.D.
Resident Surgeon.
	Richard K. Hoffman M.D.
	Attending Surgeon.
